About

Thank you for visiting my site!

I am a self taught software developer focused on building full stack Javascript Applications. Equipped with a diverse background from a degree in Electrical Engineering to creating and running a business. I enjoy thinking outside the box and building tools to solve business problems.

While I am not coding I enjoy gardening, cooking and reading books. Everyday I strive to grow and learn new things, be that the newest tech tools or old wisdom from the past. My motto is to never stop learning and leave everything in better condition than I found it.

Please don't hesitate to get in touch to see how I can help with your next project!

Based in Portland, OR
